about : emery w phillips
ux/ui designer & studio artist

Emery is a designer living and working in Atlanta. She graduated with a BFA from the Maryland Institute College of Art (MICA) in Baltimore, and completed a course in UX|UI from Georgia Tech in November of 2019.

In her spare time she enjoys aquascaping her freshwater aquarium, participating in a competitive axe-throwing league, testing new pescatarian recipes, volunteering with animal rescues, traveling the world, and reading with her cats.
